Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Career Success at UMSI

Ready to take the next step toward your future career? The UMSI Career Development Resource Hub is your one-stop shop for trusted guidance, practical tools, and the latest information to help you thrive as a student and job-seeker. Whether you're just starting your exploration or preparing to launch your career, you'll find everything you need—organized and easy to access.

Why Use This Resource?

  • Everything in One Place: Access all resources for resumes, interviews, networking, and mentorship without searching endlessly.
  • Student-Focused Navigation: Browse by topic or question—key areas are highlighted so you can start quickly.
  • Guidance Backed by Experts: All materials are reviewed by the UMSI Career Development Office, ensuring quality and reliability.
  • Always Accessible: Available anytime, anywhere—optimized for all devices.
  • Modern Support, UMSI Excellence: Benchmarked with top universities while reflecting UMSI’s unique strengths.

Connect with the UMSI Career Development Office

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) offers personalized support through appointments, events, and guides. Visit the CDO website or find us on campus to schedule a session or say hello! The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) provides individualized career support tailored to each student's professional goals. Our team works closely with students through every stage of the job search process — from exploring industries and refining career interests, to building résumés and portfolios, practicing interview skills, and navigating job offers. We offer a wide range of resources including one-on-one coaching appointments, specialized workshops, employer-hosted info sessions, networking events, and comprehensive online guides created with UMSI students in mind. Whether you are preparing application materials, researching internship opportunities, or shaping a long-term career strategy, the CDO has tools and expertise to support your journey. Students can schedule appointments through our website, attend events listed on the CDO calendar, or explore curated digital resources anytime. We also encourage you to visit us in person on campus—our team is always happy to answer questions, talk through ideas, or help you plan your next steps. Stop by to schedule a session, join an event, or simply say hello. We look forward to supporting you!
